Wigglebutt's Story

Wigglebutt is a very playful little kitty. She was found wandering around a small bookstore in North Point. The owners of the bookstore let her sleep inside and play with their customers. Wigglebutt enjoys patrolling the aisles and taking naps on stacks of books.

She loves hanging out with customers especially when they are trying to read. Although she loves being around books, she gets cold sometimes in the Winter and is looking for a warm human to snuggle with.
